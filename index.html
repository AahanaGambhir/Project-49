Fun Facts about Flying Cicada

1. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all male cicada.
2. Most have red-orange eyes.
3. Females may be attracted to the sound of motors.
4. Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.

Fun Facts about Panda

1. Giant pands are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
2. Pandas go from pink to white and black(or brown).
3. Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.
4. An adult can eat 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day.
